05

Typography

Four roles in the universal {role}{Size} pattern. Oswald is the condensed display face — chrome, headings, tabular labels. Proxima Nova (Source Sans 3 as the open substitute) handles all running prose and form copy. Mono is the system stack, reserved for numerics ≥ 18px and metadata.
