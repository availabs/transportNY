TSMO at NYSDOT.

TSMO is how New York gets more out of the roads it already owns — clearing incidents faster, managing work zones smarter, and measuring all of it. These dashboards turn 14 billion speed records and a decade of event data into the statewide operations picture. What is TSMO?
