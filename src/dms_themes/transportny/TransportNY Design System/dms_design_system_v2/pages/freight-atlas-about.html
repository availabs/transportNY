About & The Plan.

The Atlas is the interactive companion to the 2024 New York State Freight Plan. Here's the plan itself — six goals, twenty strategies — the full report library, what changed since 2019, and how to get involved.

// 01

Six goals

Safety

Reduce freight-related fatalities & serious injuries

Mobility

Reliable, efficient movement of goods

Condition

State of good repair across modes

Environment

Cut emissions; build resilience (CLCPA)

Economy

Competitiveness & freight-reliant jobs

Stewardship

Sound investment, partnership & data

20 strategies across 5 focus areas — Planning & Information Sharing, Operations & Maintenance, Technology, System Investment, and Engagement & Collaboration — implement the goals, each mapped to the six in a matrix. The Atlas itself is Strategy 3 (centralized data platform) and Strategy 18 (public education).

About

The NYS Freight Atlas is produced by the New York State Department of Transportation in partnership with the University at Albany's Albany Visualization and Informatics Lab (AVAIL), which the 2024 plan names as the team modernizing the Freight Web Atlas. The Atlas turns the plan's analysis into an interactive, downloadable, metadata-rich public platform for the state's MPOs, researchers, and residents.
