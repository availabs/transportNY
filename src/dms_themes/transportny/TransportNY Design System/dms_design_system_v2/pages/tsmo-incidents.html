construction vs crash delay

Construction is New York's largest source of attributed delay by a wide margin — 188.6M vehicle-hours since 2018, twenty-two times the delay attributed to crashes. That story has its own dashboard.

Method. Event counts and categories come from TRANSCOM's multi-state feed, filtered to New York. Delay is attributed per event by joining its affected TMCs against NPMRDS probe speeds and subtracting the recurrent baseline; dollars apply FHWA's per-person-hour value of time. Clearance thresholds are computed only on events with real lanes-open timestamps (42.7%) — coverage is published alongside. Full definitions, formulas, and caveats: Data & Methodology.
